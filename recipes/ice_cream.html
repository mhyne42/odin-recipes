Strawberry Ice Cream

Description:

Strawberry ice cream is super easy to make with this recipe that doesn't use an egg-custard base but still tastes rich and creamy.

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries
  • Milk and Cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla

Steps:

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Transfer the mixture to teh freezer bowl of an ice cream maker
  3. Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze until firm
